Paired bootstrap significance vs \textit{always-Maverick}:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Learning-only: $\Delta=+0.02265$, 95\% CI [0.01438, 0.03168], $p<0.001$.
  \item Uncertainty fallback (70B): $\Delta=+0.01789$, 95\% CI [0.00663, 0.02965], $p=0.002$.
  \item Uncertainty fallback (Maverick): $\Delta=+0.01316$, 95\% CI [0.00767, 0.01945], $p<0.001$.
  \item Hybrid rule + learning: $\Delta=+0.01264$, 95\% CI [0.00030, 0.02540], $p=0.044$.
\end{itemize}

Oracle-gap ordering (best non-oracle): Learning-only (regret 0.02452), then uncertainty fallback 70B (0.02928), then uncertainty fallback Maverick (0.03401).
